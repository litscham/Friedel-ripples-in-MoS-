\section{Objective}
Complete DFT study of Mo vacancies with emphasis on LDOS mapping for STEM-EELS interpretation.

\section{Tasks}
\begin{itemize}
    \item Create supercells with Mo vacancies
    \item Optimize atomic geometry with/without symmetry constraints
    \item Calculate electronic structure and band structure
    \item Map LDOS around vacancy site
    \item Analyze charge density redistribution
    \item Study multiple supercell sizes for convergence
\end{itemize}

\section{Deliverable}
Mo vacancy results: relaxed geometries, band structures, LDOS maps, energy data, and charge density analysis.
