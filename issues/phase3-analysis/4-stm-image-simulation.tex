\section{Objective}
Generate simulated STEM-EELS spectra and maps from computed LDOS.

\section{Tasks}
\begin{itemize}
    \item Implement STEM imaging simulator for ADF (Annular Dark Field) contrast
    \item Implement EELS simulator for core-loss and low-loss spectroscopy
    \item Convert LDOS to simulated STEM-EELS signals
    \item Vary electron probe energy and collection angles
    \item Compare simulated signals with theoretical patterns
    \item Generate publication-quality energy-filtered maps
\end{itemize}

\section{Deliverable}
Simulated STEM-EELS images and spectra for all defect types with comparison to experimental data.
