\section{Objective}
Assess experimental observability of Friedel ripples via STEM-EELS.

\section{Tasks}
\begin{itemize}
    \item Analyze required STEM spatial resolution for ripple imaging
    \item Study electron beam energy effects on contrast
    \item Estimate signal-to-noise requirements for EELS measurements
    \item Review experimental STEM-EELS capabilities in literature
    \item Propose optimal experimental conditions (beam energy, collection angle, dwell time)
    \item Compare detection feasibility across different STEM operation modes
\end{itemize}

Experimental feasibility assessment document with technical recommendations and specifications.
